Navruz

Navruz is one of the most tresuared holidays in Uzbekistan. Sometimes known as New year. Navruz is a chance to celebrate nature, to wellcome a new year, and to enjoy flavorful food with friends, family and neibords. 

Navruz is celebrated a cross Western Asia and Central Asia, all the way to the Balkans.
Navrus is originated in Khorasan in Nearthen Iran, about 3000 years old.
Navruz is celebrated on the spring equanox, which is usually March 21.
